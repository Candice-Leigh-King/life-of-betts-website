Nestled in the heart of the Waterberg plateau in Limpopo, South Africa, lies a hidden gem awaiting discovery: AfriCamps Waterberg. If you're seeking a luxurious safari glamping experience surrounded by nature's splendour, look no further.

Imagine waking up to the serene sounds of the bushveld, stepping outside your luxury tent onto a veranda with breathtaking views, and indulging in a wood-fired hot tub under the starlit African sky. At AfriCamps Waterberg, this dream becomes a delightful reality.

A Glance at the Accommodation

AfriCamps Waterberg offers ten fully equipped boutique glamping tents, each meticulously designed to provide comfort amidst the wilderness. These tents boast a seamless blend of luxury and rustic charm, accommodating up to five people across two bedrooms.

Inside, you'll find a full kitchen, a cozy living and dining area with an indoor fireplace, and a private en suite bathroom featuring a spacious rain shower. The bedrooms are adorned with luxurious extra-length queen-size beds, complete with electric blankets for those cooler evenings. And let's not forget the outdoor wood-fired hot tubs - the epitome of relaxation after a day of adventure.

Activities to Delight Every Adventurer

Whether you're seeking thrilling escapades or tranquil moments in nature, AfriCamps Waterberg has something for everyone. Embark on horseback game viewing safaris or guided game drives to encounter a variety of wildlife, including giraffes, zebras, and buffaloes.

For the avid hiker, explore the safari hiking trails and immerse yourself in the beauty of the unspoiled wilderness. At night, marvel at the wonders of the cosmos during the astronomy shows, hosted by camp owner, Dr. Phillip Calcott.

If you're craving cultural immersion, partake in the 'Culture Tour' of a nearby African village or the 'Step into the Past Tour,' featuring visits to historical landmarks such as the Davidson House and St. John's Church.

Indulge in Culinary Delights

At AfriCamps Waterberg, convenience meets culinary excellence. Opt for braai or breakfast baskets, delivered straight to your tent, brimming with delicious goodies. So you can spend less time cooking and more time savoring the flavors of South Africa.

Location and Surroundings

Situated in the picturesque Waterberg plateau, AfriCamps Waterberg offers unparalleled vistas of open plains and distant mountains. This natural haven, characterized by its pristine landscapes and abundant wildlife, invites you to unwind and reconnect with nature.

En route to the camp, you'll pass through Vaalwater, the gateway to the Waterberg. Here, you can experience traditional South African hospitality at local restaurants and shops, reflecting the region's charm and warmth.

Plan Your Escape to AfriCamps Waterberg

Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway, a family adventure, or a solo retreat, AfriCamps Waterberg promises an unforgettable experience. With its luxurious amenities, diverse activities, and idyllic surroundings, it's the perfect destination to rejuvenate mind, body, and soul.
