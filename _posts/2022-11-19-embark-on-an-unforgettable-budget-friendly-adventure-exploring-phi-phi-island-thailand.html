Welcome to the tropical paradise of Phi Phi Island! Known for its stunning beaches, epic hikes, friendly locals, and mouthwatering cuisine, Phi Phi Island is the perfect destination for an unforgettable adventure that won't break the bank. It's totally possible to travel here on a strict budget!  Join us as we take you on a thrilling journey through three incredible days on Phi Phi Island, packed with adventure, relaxation, and delightful surprises. Get ready to immerse yourself in the beauty of this island gem while we share the costs in Baht, Rand, and United States Dollar value.

  1. Ferry from Phuket to Phi Phi - 550 Baht per person ($16.50) or R275 â›´ðŸŒŠ Our adventure began with a scenic ferry ride from Phuket to Phi Phi Island. Cruising through the turquoise waters of the Andaman Sea, we marveled at the breathtaking views along the way. The ferry ride cost 550 Baht per person or approximately R275 ($16.50), setting the tone for the mesmerizing experience awaiting us.
  2. Phi Phi Dream - Your Budget-Friendly Oasis - 500 Baht per night ($15) or R250 We checked into the delightful Phi Phi Dream, a budget-friendly hotel that exceeded our expectations. With private rooms, clean bathrooms, and a refreshing fan to keep us cool, it was the perfect sanctuary after a day of exploration. The best part? It's conveniently located close to everything, allowing us to easily access the island's attractions. All this comfort came at a mere 500 Baht per night or approximately R250 ($15).
  3. Food and Drink - A Gastronomic Adventure on a Budget - 200-300 Baht per day ($6-9) or R100-150 together Phi Phi Island tantalizes the taste buds with its vibrant culinary scene. We discovered our go-to spot, Hot Chilli Bar, where we indulged in delicious and affordable veggie Pad Thai for only 60 Baht or approximately R30 ($1.80). To quench our thirst, we sipped on refreshing drinks from Cha Chang, priced at a delightful 30 Baht or approximately R15 ($0.90). Remember, dining on the islands can be slightly pricier, so budget accordingly while relishing the delectable Thai flavors.
  4. Private Longtail Boat Tour - Unleash Your Inner Explorer - 1500 Baht ($45) or R750 One of the highlights of our journey was a private longtail boat tour that took us to the awe-inspiring Maya Bay, Monkey Beach, Viking Caves, and Pi Leh Bay. With crystal-clear waters surrounding us and the wind in our hair, we felt like true adventurers exploring hidden gems. The cost for this extraordinary experience was 1500 Baht or approximately R750 ($45). If you're traveling with a group or looking for a more budget-friendly option, you can join a group tour for around 400 Baht per person or approximately R200 ($12). We went with Krabi Phi.Phi Don Tour. 
  5. Phi Phi Viewpoint - Reach New Heights of Amazement - 30 Baht per person ($0.90) or R15 Embrace your inner adventurer and conquer the short but steep hike to Phi Phi Viewpoint. For a small fee of 30 Baht or approximately R15 ($0.90), we were rewarded with jaw-dropping panoramic views of the island. It's an Instagram-worthy spot that will leave you breathless and in awe of Phi Phi's natural splendor.

Phi Phi Island is a budget traveler's dream come true, offering an abundance of adventure, relaxation, and enchanting experiences without breaking the bank. With a total cost of 5610 Baht or approximately R2805 ($168), you can embark on an unforgettable journey, immersing yourself in stunning beaches, epic hikes, the warmth of the locals, and a gastronomic extravaganza. Phi Phi Island beckons you to explore its wonders while keeping your wallet happy. So, pack your bags, and get ready for an adventure of a lifetime in this island paradise.
