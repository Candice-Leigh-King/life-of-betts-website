Looking for a an Eco Villa in Canggu Bali?

Nestled amidst the verdant beauty of Canggu, Bali, Desa Hay is a hidden gem offering a complete nature escape for conscious travelers seeking serenity and sustainability. This enchanting retreat features stunning private villas, each complete with a private pool and a host of luxurious amenities, making it the perfect destination for those looking to unwind and connect with nature. In this blog, we'll explore the eco-friendly ethos of Desa Hay and what makes it a sustainable luxury getaway.

Upon entering Desa Hay, you'll be greeted by the lush, jungle gardens that surround the resort. The heart of this tranquil sanctuary lies in its six elegant villas, each thoughtfully designed to provide a sense of modern luxury. The spacious rooms feature huge beds, air conditioning, a well-stocked mini fridge, tea and coffee facilities, a nature-inspired shower, an outdoor private bath, a TV, and high-speed WiFi. Every aspect of these villas is designed to ensure your utmost comfort and relaxation.

\_

One of the standout features of Desa Hay's villas is the private pool that comes with each one. Imagine starting your day with a refreshing dip in your secluded pool or unwinding in the evening as you watch the sunset. The sound of flowing water and the lush green surroundings create an atmosphere of pure tranquility.

Desa Hay goes above and beyond to enhance your stay. As a guest, you'll enjoy a delicious breakfast that can be served as a floating breakfast right in your private swimming pool. This immersive dining experience allows you to savor your meal while basking in the natural beauty of your surroundings.

What sets Desa Hay apart is its deep commitment to sustainability. This eco-conscious resort operates on a foundation of responsible practices and a genuine love for the environment. Notably, Desa Hay boasts 105 solar panels that provide electricity to the villas, reducing its carbon footprint significantly. Additionally, the resort employs local staff and contractors, sources local produce and materials, and meticulously evaluates every amenity for its plastic content to minimize waste. These efforts align perfectly with the values of the conscious traveler.

Desa Hay in Canggu Bali offers a complete nature escape, combining luxury, sustainability, and tranquility in perfect harmony. This unique sanctuary provides spacious and elegant villas nestled within lush, private gardens, ensuring that you'll have an unforgettable experience. Whether you're lounging by your private pool, enjoying a floating breakfast, or simply soaking in the natural beauty that surrounds you, Desa Hay offers a sustainable luxury getaway that conscious travelers will cherish.
