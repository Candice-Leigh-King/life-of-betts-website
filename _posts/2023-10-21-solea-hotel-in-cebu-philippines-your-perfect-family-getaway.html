Family friendly gem in the Philippines!

If you're planning a trip to the beautiful Philippines, make sure to save this incredible gem for your next vacation: Solea Hotel in Cebu. Our first stay in the Philippines was at Solea Hotel in Mactan, Cebu, and it was nothing short of spectacular. With a plethora of amenities and activities to keep you entertained, Solea Hotel is the perfect destination for families, couples, and friends alike. In this blog, we'll take you on a virtual tour of this amazing resort, complete with details about its features and experiences that will make your visit unforgettable.

The Resort

Solea Hotel in Cebu, also known as Solea Mactan Resort, offers a tropical paradise that is hard to resist. Situated on the picturesque island of Mactan in the heart of Cebu, this resort boasts an array of fantastic amenities to cater to every age group and interest.

1. Pools Galore

One of the highlights of Solea Hotel is its collection of five incredible pools. Whether you're seeking relaxation or adventure, you'll find the perfect pool to match your mood. Here's a glimpse of what you can expect:

  • Inflatable Obstacle Course: Dive into excitement with Solea's inflatable obstacle course right in the pool. Challenge your balance and agility as you navigate this fun course.
  • Water Park: The water park offers endless splashes and giggles with water features and attractions, making it a fantastic place for both kids and the young at heart.
  • Water Slides: Get your adrenaline pumping with the thrilling water slides that will have you racing to the bottom and climbing back up for more.
  • Infinity Pool: For those seeking tranquility, the infinity pool offers breathtaking views of the sea and is perfect for a peaceful swim.
  • Children-Friendly Pools: With dedicated pools for the little ones, the resort ensures that everyone in the family can enjoy the water safely.

2. Entertainment and Activities

Aside from the pools, Solea Hotel provides a variety of activities and entertainment options. You'll find a games room and playroom where you can challenge your friends and family to fun and exciting games.

3. Private Beach

The resort also boasts a beautiful private beach, perfect for sunbathing and enjoying the clear blue waters of the Philippines. The beach is an ideal spot to watch the stunning sunset, creating unforgettable memories.

4. Dining and Bars

Solea Hotel takes care of your dining needs with an on-site restaurant offering a range of delectable dishes. Additionally, there's a rooftop bar where you can unwind with a cocktail while gazing at the panoramic sea view.

5. On-site Spa

For those seeking relaxation and pampering, the on-site spa offers a range of rejuvenating treatments, ensuring you leave feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.

6. Water Adventures

If you're an adventurer at heart, the resort provides kayaks and stand-up paddleboards to explore the surrounding waters. You can take in the stunning scenery while engaging in water sports.

7. Delicious Breakfast Buffet

A great way to start your day at Solea Hotel is with a sumptuous breakfast included in your stay. The buffet spread is not only diverse but also incredibly delicious.

A Family-Friendly Paradise

Solea Hotel in Cebu is a family-friendly haven with an abundance of activities for everyone. The resort's water features, play areas, and child-friendly pools make it an ideal choice for a family vacation. Kids and adults alike will have a blast with the various options available.

Couples looking for a romantic getaway will also find Solea Hotel to be the perfect destination. The breathtaking sunrise and sunset views, coupled with the serene gardens, provide an intimate setting for those seeking quality time together.

Friends traveling together can enjoy the excitement of the water slides, inflatable obstacle courses, and a lively atmosphere that encourages camaraderie and fun.

Solea Hotel in Cebu, Philippines, offers an array of attractions and activities that cater to diverse interests. With its stunning sea views, contemporary rooms, and value-for-money pricing, it's a must-visit destination. Whether you're planning a family getaway, a romantic escape, or a trip with friends, Solea Hotel provides the perfect setting for an unforgettable experience.
