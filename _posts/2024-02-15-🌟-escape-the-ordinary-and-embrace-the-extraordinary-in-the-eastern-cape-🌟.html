Rockdell Lodge is a hidden gem nestled in the heart of the Eastern Cape bush, offering an unforgettable safari experience. Just 100km from East London, with Stutterheim (35km) and Komga (50km) as the closest towns, Rockdell Lodge is your gateway to nature's wonders.

🦓🦒 Picture this: stunning accommodations complete with outdoor showers, luxury bathtubs, and game viewing right from your bed! It's a wildlife enthusiast's dream come true. 🌿

💫 Intimate and cosy, Rockdell offers a personal touch to your bush getaway. Step onto the spacious veranda and sip on a cuppa while observing giraffes, buffaloes, and more grazing just a stone's throw away. It's pure tranquillity, away from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. 🍃

🏡 Choose from the selection of beautifully appointed rooms, tailored to accommodate your every need. Whether you're seeking a romantic retreat, a family adventure, or a corporate gathering, Rockdell Lodge has you covered. With three family rooms and two double rooms, it can host up to 16 guests comfortably.

🍽️ Indulge in delicious cuisine during your stay at Rockdell Lodge. Opt for a self-catered experience and make use of the fully equipped kitchen facilities, or let the dedicated team pamper you with mouthwatering meals. Don't forget to add a memorable game drive to your itinerary for the ultimate safari experience! 🚙

🛣️ Please note, access to Rockdell Lodge requires a high-clearance vehicle, and when it rains, a 4x4 is recommended for safe passage. But trust us, the journey is worth it once you arrive at this slice of paradise in the Eastern Cape. 🌄
