Luxury Glamping Tents on the doorstep of Kruger National Park

🏕️ Located just 10 minutes from Kruger National Park, Summerfields Farm Lodge offers a stunning 5-star retreat on the banks of the Sabie River. Each tent boasts INCREDIBLE views, air conditioning, super comfortable beds, outdoor showers, and baths with a view!

🍽️ Indulge in luxury with daily room servicing, turndown with a nightcap, and sumptuous breakfasts included in your stay. The on-site restaurant serves up delicious meals to satisfy your cravings after a day of adventure in Kruger.

🏊‍♀️ After exploring the wonders of Kruger, take a dip in the swimming pool overlooking the Sabie River—keep your eyes peeled for crocodiles and hippos! 🦛 Need to unwind? Visit the forest spa and let the wonderful masseuses melt away all your stress. 🧖‍♂️♥️

🌿 Summerfields Farm Lodge is more than just a place to stay—it's an experience like no other. With 11 secluded tented suites, a luxury suited tent, and a forest suite, you're sure to find your perfect retreat in Hazyview. Glamorous glamping awaits you, 5-star style!

🍃 Explore the nearby attractions of the Panorama Route and indulge in activities like Kruger Park safaris and panoramic tours. There's never a dull moment at Summerfields Farm Lodge!

🌹 Experience the magic of nature at River Lodge, where 12 luxury tented suites await you. Surrounded by the tranquil sounds of cascading water and bird calls, each suite offers attention to detail and comfort. Relax in a rose-scented bath with optional sparkling wine and homemade treats, and enjoy amenities like air conditioning, large bath sheets, bath robes, and more.

🍳 Breakfast at the fully licensed River Café is a treat not to be missed, with views over the Sabie River adding to the ambiance. Plus, enjoy free WiFi in the rooms to stay connected during your stay.

🌟 Your Kruger getaway awaits at Summerfields Farm Lodge—book your adventure today and immerse yourself in luxury and nature! 🦏🦛🦁🐾
