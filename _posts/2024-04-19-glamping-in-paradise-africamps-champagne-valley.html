A Look at AfriCamps Champagne Valley

Picture waking up to the crisp mountain air, surrounded by panoramic views of the Champagne Valley and the majestic Drakensberg Mountains. AfriCamps Champagne Valley offers precisely that and more. Nestled on the foothills of the Drakensberg, this glamping retreat combines the comforts of luxury camping with the tranquility of nature. If you're seeking the perfect getaway to the Drakensberg to see the snow capped mountains in winter, this might be the perfect spot.

Luxurious Tents with a View

The tents at AfriCamps Champagne Valley redefine camping luxury. Each tent comfortably sleeps five people, with two double beds and one single, ensuring ample space for families or groups of friends. Electric blankets and a heater/air conditioner guarantee cozy nights, while the indoor fire and outdoor braai set the scene for memorable evenings under the stars. Plus each tent has a private wood-fired hottub overlooking the Champagne Valley.

Adventure Awaits

Beyond the comfort of your tent, a world of adventure beckons! Explore the on site eco pool for a refreshing swim, or head out on a scenic walking trail through the forest. Thrill-seekers will find plenty to do, from birding and mountain biking to hot air ballooning and ziplining. And for those craving a cultural experience, don‚Äôt miss the chance to hear the renowned Drakensberg Boys Choir perform live!

Things to do in Champagne Valley

Champagne Valley, nestled within the Central Drakensberg, is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. Home to the highest peaks in South Africa, including Cathkin Peak and Champagne Castle, this UNESCO World Heritage Site boasts unparalleled hiking, mountain biking, and rock climbing opportunities. Whether you seek adrenaline-pumping adventures or peaceful moments in nature, Champagne Valley has it all. Make sure to pop into the Valley Bakery for delicious treats, Hillbillies for local produce, the Drakensberg Chocolate Memories for chocolate tasting, The Ugly Duckling for everything and anything, and Thokozisa Centre to do some retail therapy.

Conveniently located just 245 km from Durban and 400 km from Johannesburg, AfriCamps Champagne Valley is the perfect weekend or holiday destination.
