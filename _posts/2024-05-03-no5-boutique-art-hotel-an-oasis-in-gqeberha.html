If you're looking for a luxurious getaway that combines art, comfort, and exceptional service, No5 Boutique Art Hotel by Mantis is the place to be. Nestled in the affluent Summerstrand suburb of Gqeberha, South Africa, this charming art-deco hotel offers a unique experience for both business and leisure travellers.

Comfort and Luxury

No5 Boutique Art Hotel features ten spacious suites designed for comfort. Each suite comes with a large bath and walk-in shower, tea and coffee facilities, a TV lounge, and a small balcony. Whether you’re relaxing after a long day or enjoying your morning coffee, these suites provide a cosy and stylish retreat.

Amenities for Every Taste

The hotel’s amenities cater to every need. Take a dip in the large swimming pool or treat yourself at the on-site spa, TITE Medi Spa by Camelot. If you’re a movie buff, the private cinema offers an exclusive viewing experience. The underground dining in the wine cellar is a unique culinary adventure, while the fine dining restaurant ensures every meal is special. The whiskey and cigar lounge is ideal for relaxing with a touch of elegance.

Art and Culture

No5 Boutique Art Hotel is home to a private art collection, with incredible pieces displayed throughout the hotel. Guests can explore these artworks with the help of a guidebook provided in each suite, making for a culturally enriching stay.

Ideal Location

Located less than 10 minutes from Chief Dawid Stuurman International Airport, No5 is easy to reach yet feels like a peaceful retreat. Just a short walk from the ocean, the hotel’s location allows guests to enjoy the calm coastal atmosphere while still being close to the city’s attractions.

A Place to Unwind

No5 Boutique Art Hotel by Mantis sets a high standard for luxury accommodation in Gqeberha. Its mix of South African art, excellent facilities, and great service makes it a special place to stay. Whether you want to relax, explore, or just enjoy some luxury, No5 offers an experience that’s both unique and memorable.
