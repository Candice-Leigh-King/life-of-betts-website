Just a quick 20-minute drive from Gqeberha airport, Nyosi Wildlife Reserve is a hidden gem offering a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Nestled between two cities, this peri-urban reserve is a unique blend of urban vibrancy and the wild heart of Africa.

Cozy Accommodations with Breathtaking Views

Nyosi Wildlife Reserve provides stunning self-catering cabins, each thoughtfully designed to ensure a comfortable stay. The cozy bedroom, small kitchen, and living room are complemented by incredible views over the reserve. For a touch of luxury, the deluxe cabin features a private wood-fired hot tub, perfect for unwinding after a day of adventure.

A Day Packed with Activities

There's never a dull moment at Nyosi. Daily activities include two game drives where knowledgeable game rangers, Braden and Jack, ensure you get up close and personal with the wildlife. They are incredibly passionate about the animals and share their knowledge enthusiastically, making each drive an educational experience. From majestic elephants to the elusive cheetah, the wildlife encounters here are unforgettable.

For those who love to explore on foot, the nature hike with Blackie and Jack is a must. They guide you through the reserve, teaching you about the local flora and its traditional medicinal uses. The hike is not just informative but also offers the chance to spot a variety of wildlife, including a beautiful mama cheetah and her four adorable cubs.

Relax and Rejuvenate

After a morning of adventure, you can relax with a picnic offering stunning views, enjoy sundowners, or take a refreshing dip in the swimming pool. The on-site restaurant is another highlight, where Chef Tim's culinary skills will amaze you. His dishes are a delightful blend of local flavours and international cuisine, making every meal a treat.

Perfect for All Stays

Whether you're looking for a day-long excursion or an extended retreat, Nyosi has something to offer everyone. The reserve provides personalised itineraries to match your interests and timeline, ensuring a tailor-made experience. Enjoy a safari game drive, picnic in nature, or sample fine wines while gazing over the grassy fynbos.

A Unique Peri-Urban Experience

Nyosi is the first-of-its-kind peri-urban game reserve, where wildlife roams freely amidst the fresh fynbos-scented air and picturesque scenery. It's a place where humans and nature reunite to rewild the landscape, creating a harmonious environment for all.
