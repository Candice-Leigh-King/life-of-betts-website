As passionate travel and lifestyle content creators with a deep love for adventure—whether we’re exploring the rugged landscapes of South Africa or discovering new horizons abroad—this device has truly transformed the way we capture and share our stories. In this blog, we’re excited to highlight two standout features that have taken our travel content creation to the next level over the past two weeks.

First up, the FlexCam with Galaxy AI. This feature is a dream for creating travel content, thanks to its simplicity and versatility. With three shooting modes—Portrait, Photo, and Video—you can effortlessly capture stunning selfies, group photos, or videos right from the Cover screen.

The best part? Nightography compatibility ensures bright, clear photos, even in low light. Whether it’s adjusting the screen aspect ratio, setting a timer, or fine-tuning video settings, the FlexCam makes it all a breeze.

Another feature we’re loving is the Super Slow-mo. This mode lets us slow down our videos to capture every detail, making sure we get the perfect shot every time. Super Slow-mo lets us nail the perfect shot while filming in the field, ensuring we always get that perfect, crisp shot.
