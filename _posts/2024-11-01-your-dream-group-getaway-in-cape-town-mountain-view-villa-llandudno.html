What Makes Mountain View Villa So Special?

This place really has it all. With room for up to 12 guests, Mountain View Villa is the perfect spot for family gatherings, friend reunions, or a special occasion. There are six beautifully decorated bedrooms in total—five inside the main villa and one in a private garden cottage, ideal for anyone wanting a bit of extra space.

The villa’s decor takes a lot of inspiration from tropical Africa, giving each room a warm, inviting vibe. And those views? Seriously, they’re next-level—this place lives up to the "Mountain View" name with sweeping, unmatched views of the mountains.

Top-Notch Amenities

Let’s talk amenities. This villa is loaded with everything you need for a fantastic stay. Josh and I loved lounging by the private pool in the afternoons after a morning at Llandudno Beach, soaking up the mountain views in the afternoon sun. Mornings were for coffee on the deck swings, with the mountains as a perfect backdrop, and as the sun set, we’d cosy up by the indoor fireplace as the sky turned golden. Here’s a quick look at what’s included:

  • A fully equipped kitchen for the ultimate self-catering experience—perfect for cooking up group meals or even just some late-night snacks.

  • A huge, open-plan living area with tons of comfy seating and natural light, making it ideal for relaxing or socialising with everyone.

  • Outdoor entertainment options including a braai area (South African-style BBQ) on a spacious deck with comfortable seating, where you can soak in those views.

  • Private Sauna downstairs guests can enjoy a private sauna.

Exploring the Area

Mountain View Villa is just a quick drive to Llandudno Beach, a beautiful, uncrowded spot with white sand and crystal-clear water. And if you’re into hiking, you’ll find plenty of trails nearby that give you incredible views over the Atlantic coastline. Llandudno itself is quiet, private, and perfect for anyone wanting a peaceful escape while still being close to all the best bits of Cape Town!

Honestly, Mountain View Villa should be on your bucket list! It's the perfect getaway for the whole family.
