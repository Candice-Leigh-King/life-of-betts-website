One of the things I love most about our travels is how places feel completely different at night. There’s a certain stillness and mood that makes everything seem more magical. On one of our recent nights in Spain, I couldn’t resist pulling out the Samsung Galaxy Z Flip6 to capture the quiet beauty around us.

As a photographer, I’m used to paying close attention to settings and details when I’m shooting with my camera. But with a phone, I usually just point and shoot without fussing too much. What surprised me about the Z Flip6 is how easy it is to get incredible results without any extra effort.

We decided to experiment with some photos and see how the Z Flip6 would handle low light. The Night Mode made it so simple—everything was crisp and clear, even though it was quite dark. When I wanted a little more control, I switched over to Pro Photo Mode and adjusted things like the brightness and shutter speed. It was great to have the option to tweak things but also to rely on the automatic settings if I just wanted to enjoy the moment.

One feature I absolutely loved was the FlexCam. Instead of trying to hold the phone steady or prop it up awkwardly, I folded the Z Flip6 halfway and set it down for perfectly steady shots. It gave me the freedom to experiment with angles and composition without worrying about balancing the phone.

The hands-free setup let me focus on the framing, and it was amazing how much variety I could get just by changing the angle slightly. It’s the kind of versatility that really makes a difference when you’re shooting on the go!

When I started editing the photos later, I noticed one of my favourites was slightly skew. Normally, I’d just leave it as is, but the Photo Assist tool made fixing it so easy. Using the Generative Edit, I straightened the image and enlarged it without losing any quality.

This tool is such a game-changer—it’s perfect for quick fixes like resizing, moving objects, or even removing distractions.

What stood out most about using the Samsung Galaxy Z Flip6 was how it made nighttime photography so effortless. The ProVisual Engine brought out the details I thought would be lost in the shadows, and the built-in AI tools took the stress out of editing.

As someone who usually doesn’t expect much from phone photography, I was genuinely impressed. The Z Flip6 isn’t just convenient—it’s a tool that genuinely delivers great results.

If you’ve ever felt like night photography was too tricky, I’d highly recommend giving this phone a try. It’s such an easy way to create something beautiful, even in low light. 🌙
