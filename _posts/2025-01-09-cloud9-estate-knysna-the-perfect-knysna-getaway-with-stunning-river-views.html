Looking for a unique and relaxing spot for your next Knysna getaway? Cloud9 Estate Knysna is exactly what you need! This stunning 5-sleeper container cabin is perched high above the Knysna River, offering the kind of views you’ll be dreaming about long after you leave. Think lush mountains, shimmering waters, and total tranquillity.

The cabin itself is modern, cosy, and has everything you need for a comfortable stay. With two spacious bedrooms (one with an en suite and the other with a separate bathroom), it’s perfect for families or a small group of friends. Plus, there’s a sleeper couch if you’re bringing along an extra guest. The fully equipped kitchen makes self-catering a breeze, and there’s an indoor fireplace for those cosy evenings in. But let’s be honest, you’ll probably spend most of your time outside—and for good reason!

The cabin’s patio is a dream with a gas braai, outdoor dining area, and even a dartboard to keep the fun going. And if you’re a fan of a good old South African braai, the fire boma is the perfect spot to gather around under the stars.

When you’re ready to explore, Knysna town is just a short drive away. There are plenty of hiking trails, cafes, and shops to keep you busy. One of our top recommendations? Grab a takeaway coffee and head to Pledge Nature Reserve. It’s just a quick 10-minute walk to the viewpoint, and trust us, the views are totally worth it. It’s a solid 10/10 experience.

Cloud9 Estate is the perfect mix of peace, comfort, and convenience. Whether you’re coming for a romantic escape, a family getaway, or a trip with friends, this spot ticks all the boxes. So, who would you bring along for this dreamy Knysna escape? Let us know!
