Some places just have that perfect balance of cosy comfort and outdoor adventure, and Nguni Cabin at Penwarn Farm Lodge is one of them. If you're looking for a family-friendly getaway with plenty of space to roam, this hidden gem near Underberg is exactly what you need.

The cabin sleeps five people across two bedrooms—one with a double bed, and the other with two ¾ beds and a single—making it an ideal spot for families or small groups. Inside, you'll find a fully equipped kitchen, a comfy living room with an indoor fireplace, and a patio with a braai area and seating that overlooks the dam. It’s the kind of place where you can start your morning with a cup of coffee on the deck, watching the mist roll over the water, before heading out for a day full of adventures.

Penwarn Farm Lodge is all about embracing the outdoors. With multiple dams on the property, guests can enjoy kayaking, fishing, or simply soaking in the peaceful scenery. There are several hiking trails, including a waterfall trailand a Bushman painting trail, plus natural mountain pools for a refreshing swim on warm days. The jungle gym nearby makes it even more perfect for families with little ones.

Nguni Cabin is the kind of place that feels like a home away from home—except with breathtaking views, endless outdoor activities, and that peaceful, off-the-grid charm. Spend your mornings kayaking on the dam, afternoons hiking to waterfalls, and evenings by the fire, completely surrounded by nature. It’s the perfect mix of adventure and relaxation, making it an unforgettable spot for a family getaway. Would this make it onto your 2025 bucket list? Let us know!
