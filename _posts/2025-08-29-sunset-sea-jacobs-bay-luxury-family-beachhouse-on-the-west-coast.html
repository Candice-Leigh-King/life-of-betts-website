If you’re craving a beach escape that has something for the whole family, look no further than Sunset Sea in Jacobs Bay. This gorgeous luxury beachhouse is tucked along South Africa’s stunning West Coast, surrounded by wildflowers and set right on the water’s edge. It’s the kind of place where days are filled with salty swims, barefoot walks, and endless ocean views.

The house itself is designed for families and groups of friends, comfortably sleeping 8 guests in 4 spacious bedrooms - all fitted with electric blankets to keep things cozy when the evenings turn cool. A full kitchen makes self-catering easy, and the indoor fireplace adds that touch of warmth for relaxed nights in. Outside, a wide patio looks out over the ocean, complete with a heated pool, braai area, hammock, and an outdoor dining table perfect for long, lazy meals.

What makes Sunset Sea so much fun is the mix of relaxation and activity. You’ll have access to a kayak for paddling in the bay, a foosball table and darts for some healthy competition, and even a small bowling green that keeps everyone entertained. Right in front of the house are rockpools waiting to be explored, while a swimming beach is just a short stroll away.

The location couldn’t be better. Jacobs Bay is a quiet, charming coastal town where life slows down. From the house you can wander into town, or set out on the hiking trail that starts right outside the gate. The best part? Sunset Sea is pet friendly, so you don’t have to leave anyone behind. Bring the whole family - four-legged friends included - and soak up the beauty of South Africa’s West Coast in comfort and style.
