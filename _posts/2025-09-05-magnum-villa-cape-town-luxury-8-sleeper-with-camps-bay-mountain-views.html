If you’ve ever wanted to wake up in a villa with views of Camps Bay, Lion’s Head, and the Twelve Apostles all in one sweep, then Magnum Villa by NOX should be at the very top of your Cape Town wishlist. This exceptionally designed 8-sleeper property in Bakoven offers everything you could want from a luxury group getaway - space, style, comfort, and the kind of views you’ll never forget.

Priced from R8400 per night for 8 people, Magnum Villa is a dream for families or friends travelling together. The house has four spacious bedrooms, each designed with relaxation in mind. There’s a full kitchen for cooking up feasts, two separate living rooms for spreading out, and both indoor and outdoor dining areas so you can enjoy every meal with a view.

The outdoor spaces are made for Cape Town’s sunshine-filled days. A large swimming pool sparkles in the centre of the patio, surrounded by loungers and seating areas that make it perfect for lazy afternoons or sundowner gatherings. There’s a braai and firepit for evenings under the stars, and a private gym for those who want to keep active during their stay. Every detail of the villa has been thought through to give you the ultimate balance of luxury and comfort.

Of course, the real highlight is the location. Magnum Villa sits in prime position, overlooking Camps Bay’s famous beachfront while being backed by the dramatic Twelve Apostles mountain range. Whether you’re sipping coffee at sunrise or clinking glasses at sunset, the views from this villa are simply spectacular. It’s the kind of place that makes you pause just to take it all in - and a reminder of why Cape Town is one of the most beautiful cities in the world.

For a group stay that combines world-class luxury with unbeatable scenery, Magnum Villa by NOX is everything you could want and more. If you’re planning a trip to Cape Town with friends or family, this is one stay worth saving up for.
