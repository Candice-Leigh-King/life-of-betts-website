If you’ve ever dreamed of waking up to the sound of waves crashing just beyond your doorstep, with sweeping views of mountains and sea, then Solmara House in Llandudno might just be your new favourite holiday address.

Tucked into one of Cape Town’s most exclusive coastal suburbs, this five-bedroom luxury villa offers direct access to the iconic Blue Flag swimming beach of Llandudno - a pristine stretch of white sand framed by giant boulders and turquoise Atlantic waters.

Step inside and you’re greeted with a sense of space and warmth. Five spacious bedrooms ensure everyone has their own slice of comfort, while two living rooms plus an additional TV lounge make it easy to spread out or come together depending on the mood. The heart of the home beats around the indoor fireplace and bar, where laughter flows as easily as a good glass of wine. For families, the full kitchen - complete with a Nespresso coffee machine, air fryer, and all the trimmings - means you can cook up everything from easy breakfasts to long, lazy dinners. There’s even a laundry room tucked away, so extended stays feel effortless.

What makes Solmara House so special is how it brings the outside in. With indoor and outdoor dining areas, a braai, firepit, and outdoor shower, life here flows seamlessly between sunshine and shade. The showstopper is a massive swimming pool framed by the mountains on one side and ocean on the other. Picture sun-drenched afternoons drifting between dips in the pool and walks down to the beach - just steps from your door. Come evening, gather around the firepit with blankets and marshmallows, watching the sun melt into the Atlantic.

Unlike its glitzier neighbours, Llandudno feels like a secret pocket of paradise. There are no shops or restaurants in the suburb itself, keeping it wonderfully private and peaceful. Yet, you’re just a short drive from the buzzing energy of Camps Bay, Hout Bay, and Cape Town’s city centre. It’s the best of both worlds: seclusion when you want it, and vibrant dining, cafés, and adventures when you don’t.

Whether you’re planning a multi-generational family holiday, a group reunion, or a stylish getaway with friends, Solmara House has thought of everything to make your stay unforgettable. From lazy mornings with coffee overlooking the sea, to evenings spent sharing stories by the firepit, every detail feels designed to help you unwind, reconnect, and savour Cape Town’s incredible coastline.
