There’s a stretch of coastline on South Africa’s Sunshine Coast where time seems to slow, where the air smells of salt and sea spray, and where mornings begin with the sound of waves brushing the shore. Just steps from the tranquil sands of Shelly Beach and a short stroll from the ever-popular Kelly’s Beach, you’ll find Ocean Mooring Beach House - a beautifully appointed, family-friendly retreat in Port Alfred that feels like it was built for togetherness.

This expansive 14-sleeper beach house is everything a group getaway should be: open, airy, and wonderfully close to the ocean. With seven spacious bedrooms, five bathrooms, two full kitchens, and three TV lounges, it’s the kind of place where everyone has room to unwind - yet still gather in one of the shared spaces for a meal, a laugh, or a game night. Whether you’re planning a multi-generational family holiday or a group trip with friends, Ocean Mooring Beach House offers space, comfort, and that unmistakable coastal charm.

Inside, you’ll find a home designed for easy living. The kitchens are fully equipped with everything you need to prepare meals with ease - from breakfast spreads to evening braais. There’s Wi-Fi throughout, Smart TVs for movie nights, and soft, coastal-inspired décor that mirrors the calm of the ocean just beyond your window. Each bedroom feels light and inviting, with quality linen and bath towels provided to make your stay that little bit more luxurious.

Outside, the rhythm of beach life takes over. The property has two dedicated braai areas, perfect for lazy summer evenings in the salty air. The large enclosed garden is a paradise for kids - plenty of space to run, play cricket, toss a frisbee, or simply lie in the grass after a morning at the beach. The house’s proximity to the shore means that when the tide pulls out, you’re only a few steps from rock pools ready to be explored and a Blue Flag beach perfect for swimming, surfing, or sunset walks.

For those who love exploring beyond the sand, Port Alfred’s town centre is just a short drive away. You’ll find charming cafés, restaurants, and shops, as well as a laid-back coastal community that still feels refreshingly small-town. For a fun day trip, head 20 minutes inland to Bathurst, a quirky village famous for The Big Pineapple and home to The Pig and Whistle, said to be the oldest licensed pub in Africa. And for golf lovers, there’s even a golf course just up the road, offering spectacular views of the coastline as you play.

Evenings at Ocean Mooring are best spent outdoors - a slow braai, a few drinks on the patio, the sound of the waves mingling with laughter. When the day winds down, the sea breeze drifts through open doors, and the horizon fades into stars. It’s that perfect blend of comfort and freedom that defines a true beach escape. <3

It’s not just a place to stay, but one to return to again and again.
